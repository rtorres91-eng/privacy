# Privacy Policy for Crypto Bird

**Last Updated: February 16, 2026**

## Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Crypto Bird ("we", "our", or "the app") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our mobile game application.

## Information We Collect

### Information Stored Locally
- **High Scores**: Your game high scores are stored locally on your device using iOS UserDefaults
- **Game Progress**: Your selected coin preference and game settings are stored locally on your device

### Information We Do NOT Collect
- We do NOT collect any personally identifiable information
- We do NOT track your location
- We do NOT access your contacts, photos, or other personal data
- We do NOT share any data with third parties
- We do NOT require you to create an account

## How We Use Information

All game data (high scores, preferences) is stored **locally on your device only**. This data:
- Is used solely to enhance your gaming experience
- Never leaves your device
- Is not transmitted to any servers
- Is deleted when you uninstall the app

## Third-Party Services

Currently, Crypto Bird does not use any third-party services, analytics, or advertising networks.

**If we add advertising in the future**, we will update this policy and notify users. Potential ad networks may collect:
- Device identifiers
- App usage data
- Ad interaction data

## Children's Privacy

Crypto Bird does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. The game is appropriate for all ages and does not require any personal information to play.

## Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, the security of your game data is managed by your device's iOS security features. We do not have access to this data.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Delete your game data by uninstalling the app
- Contact us with any privacy concerns

## Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
